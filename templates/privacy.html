Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use QHive, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Bio, profile picture, and other information you choose to share.
  • Content: Posts, comments, messages, stories, and other content you create.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the platform, including pages visited and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the QHive platform
  • To personalize your experience and show relevant content
  • To enable communication between users
  • To send important notifications about your account
  • To improve our services and develop new features
  • To protect against fraud and abuse

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share information only in these cases:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users
  • With service providers who help us operate the platform

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure connections, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your data in your profile and settings.
  • Update: You can update your information at any time in Settings.
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data in Settings → Account.
  • Export: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences (like theme choice). We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@QHive.com
