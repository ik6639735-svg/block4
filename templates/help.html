Help Center

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click "Create one" on the login page. Enter your username, email, and password. You can also sign up with Google.

How do I create a post?

Click the "Create Post" button in the sidebar or the + button on mobile. You can add text, photos, videos, and polls.

How do I add a story?

Click "Add Story" on the home feed. Select a photo or video from your device or take one with your camera.

Features

What is QHive AI?

QHive AI is your personal trading assistant. Ask it about trading strategies, market analysis, content ideas, and more.

How do hashtags work?

Add #hashtags to your posts. Other users can click on hashtags to see all posts with that tag. Popular hashtags appear in Trending.

How do I message someone?

Go to Messages, search for a user, and start a conversation. You can also click "Message" on someone's profile.

Can I change the theme?

Yes! Go to Settings → Appearance. Choose from Dark Gold, Midnight Blue, or Light theme.

Account

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings → Password. Enter your current password and your new password.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
